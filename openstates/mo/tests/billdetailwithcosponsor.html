Current Bill Summary


SB 285 - This act removes February 12th (Lincoln's birthday) as a public holiday in the State of Missouri.

This act is similar to a provision in SS/HB 1268 (2010), SCS/HB 1677 (2010), and SCS/HB 1778 (2010).
